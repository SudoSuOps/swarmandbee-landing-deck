SwarmAtlas-27B

We don't sell models. We sell verified training data. Atlas exists to prove the data is bankable — and it did.

The Thesis

SwarmAtlas-27B was trained on 45,039 capital markets pairs from the Swarm data estate. On a live CRE deal stress test, it scored 12 out of 12 on mathematical accuracy and correctly identified the structural deal-killer that would have cost the LP their preferred return. That analysis used to take a team of brokers 10-15 days. Atlas did it in minutes.

The Validation — Memphis IC Test

A real deal. Real numbers. No synthetic prompts. Atlas was given raw deal parameters and asked to underwrite and make an investment committee recommendation.

Deal Parameters

Property: 312-unit Class B Multifamily Location: Memphis, TN Basis: $14.2M Financing: 80% LTC bridge loan @ 8.35% Exit Cap: 5.75%
